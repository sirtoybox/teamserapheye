Welcome

Be Not Afraid!

Welcome to the homepage of Team Seraph Eye! We are a collective of various artists, creatives, and hobbyists all producing independantly created works! This website serves as a massive home network for a number of affiliated creators, with links to all of them and their projects.

We will use this page to deliver you updates and news regarding many projects shared here, so feel free to take a look around and explore anything that you take an interest in!

What kinds of projects do you host?

Right now we are simply hosting projects done by the founders of this project, on top of a number of close friends, though we are interested in hosting all types of media! Examples can be seen as follows:

  • Original and Fan Comics
  • Art
  • Music
  • Videos and Livestreams
  • Edits
  • And more!

    • All projects that are hosted by Team Seraph Eye are fully owned by their respective creators. This website is at its core designed to be a network for said creators.

    Contact

    How do I contact affiliated creators?

    All creators on our website can be found at their associated links. Be wary that due to the nature of this site, some creators may not all be on the same social media pages/networks. Regardless, all work will be properly credited, and in the worst case scenario, feel free to contact our founders and ask!
